# Landing Page
The primary purpose of a landing page is to convert targeted traffic (from ads, emails, or social media) into tangible business outcomes. 

The core objectives include: 

- **Lead Generation**: Collecting visitor contact details (like and email or phone number) in exchange for a valuable offer, such as an eBook, webinar registration, or free trial.

- **Sales and Promotions**: Directing users to make an immediate purchase of a specific product, service, or limited-time offer.

- **Event Registration**: Providing event details and securing RSVPs ot ticket purchases.

- **Audience Alignment**: Matching the messaging exactly to the promise made in the advertisement that brought the visitor there, which increases trust and conversions.
